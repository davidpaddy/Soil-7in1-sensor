Sensor Output Description:

This 7-in-1 soil sensor provides critical data for agricultural and environmental research. It measures:

  • Nitrogen (N): Essential for plant growth, high levels can cause excessive foliage with delayed flowering.
  • Phosphorous (P): Important for root development, too much can harm beneficial soil microorganisms.
  • Potassium (K): Regulates plant metabolism and affects water inside and outside of plant cells.
  • Soil Moisture: Vital for plant health, soil moisture level is a key factor in water management.
  • Temperature: Soil temperature influences seed germination rates and plant growth.
  • Electrical Conductivity (EC): Indicates the amount of salts in soil, which affects nutrient availability.
  • pH Level: Affects nutrient availability and soil structure, influencing plant health.
